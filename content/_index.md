## A Flutter-based inventory management system

**Antonio's Pizza Pub Inventory System** is a comprehensive solution for tracking restaurant inventory with real-time updates, low stock alerts, and remote access capabilities.

### Key Features

- ✅ **Real-time Inventory Management** - Add, update, and remove items instantly
- ✅ **Low Stock Alerts** - Automatic warnings when items fall below threshold (3 units)
- ✅ **Search Functionality** - Quickly find items by name
- ✅ **Multiple Sort Options** - Sort by name or quantity in various orders
- ✅ **User-Friendly Interface** - Intuitive design with clear navigation
- ✅ **Remote Access** - Access inventory from anywhere when PocketBase is accessible
- ✅ **Cross-Platform** - Works on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS, and Web
